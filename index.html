How does this currency converter work?

Currency converter will show you how much your money is worth in other currencies at the real exchange rate. You can convert over currencies quickly and easily.


Simply input your amount in the currency calculator above, select your source and destination currency and our tool will convert your currency at the mid-market rate (that’s the one you’ll find on Google). Send money abroad using this and we’ll use the same rate you see on our currency converter.
